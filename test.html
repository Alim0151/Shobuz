Financial Lines Explained

Essential coverage protecting businesses and individuals against complex financial risks. Here are the cornerstones of our offering.

Directors & Officers (D&O)

Protects company officers personally if sued for decisions made in their roles (mismanagement, regulatory breaches).

View Real Scenario

A construction firm's client liquidates before payment. The firm closes, and laid-off employees sue the directors personally for lost earnings. D&O covers the directors' defense and damages.

Professional Indemnity (PII)

Covers claims of negligence, errors, or omissions in services provided to clients.

View Real Scenario

A consultant's marketing advice leads to regulatory fines for a client. The client sues the consultant for ¬£1M in lost earnings. PII covers the defense and settlement.

Corporate Liability (CPL)

Protects the business itself from wrongful acts, regulatory breaches, or shareholder disputes. Often an extension of D&O.

Employment Practices (EPL)

Covers claims from employees regarding discrimination, harassment, or wrongful termination.

Why It Matters

Financial Lines is a high-growth, high-profit sector essential for UK businesses. Its complexity often leaves clients underserved. We aim to bridge this gap with education and transparent coverage.
