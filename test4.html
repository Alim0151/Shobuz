About the Game

The year is 1989. You are Henry, a man who retreats from a tangled life to work as a fire lookout in the Wyoming wilderness. Perched high atop a tower, your job is to look for smoke and keep the forest safe.

An especially hot, dry summer puts everyone on edge. Your supervisor Delilah is your only consistent contact via a small handheld radio.

When something strange pulls you away from the tower and into the forest, you explore an atmospheric and mysterious world and make choices that shape the relationship you have with Delilah.

Beautiful visuals, measured pacing and a personal story—Firewatch is a first-person mystery about connection and attention.
